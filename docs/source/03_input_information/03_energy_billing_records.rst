Energy Billing Records
======================

These are the monthly municipal bills detailing the billing for the month.  Those with smart meters
and Ecwin login, can retrieve billing records too.

The billing information contains the consumption and cost for each time of use rate for the month
as well as the monthly peak demand, and it's charge.
